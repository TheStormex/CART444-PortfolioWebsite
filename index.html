A professional headshot is key to making a great impression! Take advantage of the free LinkedIn photoshoot with one of Concordia’s professional photographers available exclusively to Concordia students. You will be contacted prior to the event with a specific time slot and the event location. If you cannot attend, please cancel you attendance so that your spot can be given to another student. Photoshoot tips: Dress professionally: You want to look like yourself on your best day Be positive: Be enthusiastic and confident, this shines through in your photo! Relax and have fun: Follow any instructions given by the photographer for a great headshot Guidelines for clothing and styles: Wear solid colors Avoid logos, writing or graphics on clothing Clothing should represent your professionalism Due to high demand, students can partake in one photoshoot session per degree during their time at Concordia. The photoshoot sessions are not available to alumni. Please note that the final photo selection is left to the discretion of University Communication Services (UCS) who will be conducting the LinkedIn Photoshoot. UCS will select the best photo that you have taken.
