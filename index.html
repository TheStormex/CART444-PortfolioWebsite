About Me

Who am I?

I am a game designer from Montreal. I specialize in mechanics design and have created many games in college and university for which I attained high marks, such as Re-Boot, a 2D puzzle platformer about a clone-spawning robot. Currently, while finishing up his degree in Computation Arts at Concordia University, I work at the university’s Welcome Centre as a student ambassador and am lauded by my supervisor and coworkers for my dependability and interpersonal skills. My hobbies include dancing, and I was the Canadian champion of Just Dance World Cup in 2014, 2015, and 2019.
